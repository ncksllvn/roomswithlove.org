We accept the following __gently used__ items.

## Living rooms
- Couches (no sofa beds)
- Love seats
- End tables
- Coffee tables
- Lamps

## Kitchens
- Kitchen tables
- Chairs
- Microwaves
- Dishes
- Utensils
- Coffee makers

## Bedrooms
- Lamps
- Nightstands
- Dressers
- Beds (queen-size and under)
